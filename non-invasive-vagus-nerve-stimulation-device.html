Project for a rehabilitation center for stroke patients. Worked with multiple teams to develop a non invasive vagus nerve stimulation device that would be activated just when the exercise performed by the patient was done correctly through infrared technology. Eliminating the need of a therapist and an implanted device, as well as allowing the patient to perform the exercises more often from home.
