Cystoscope

Created a patient friendly low cost device for injection molding

Date: Fall 2020
Institution: Duke University
Skills: Manufacturing Techniques,
FEA Analysis, Photoshop, Fusion360

Left Click to Rotate | Scroll to Zoom | Right Click to Pan

PART 1: Widget — Photon Engine

A 3D printed widget and a caliper were mailed to us to first, make an sketch to scale. The drawings were made in 3rd angle projection (ASME).

Later to be modeled in Autodesk Fusion 360, and redesigned to fit and be secured inside a cystoscope that we would also need to design.

PART 2: Cystoscope

Requirements:

  1. Must accommodate securely, mount and protect the widget
  2. Must provide an in/out water connection
  3. Must provide provision to control water in/out flow
  4. Must accommodate 24F sheath 20 cm in length
  5. Must suit left or right handed use
  6. Electrical connections
  7. Manufacturable by injection molding
